# How We Deliver It — AI-Powered Sales

## Engagement Model

Every engagement ends up in the same place — an AI-powered sales system running on HubSpot with an ongoing retainer to keep it sharp. How we get there depends on the customer's starting point.

**Path A — Full implementation (new to HubSpot or migrating)**
A single integrated project covering HubSpot setup and AI-powered sales configuration together. Delivered in phases over 6–10 weeks, followed by a monthly retainer. HubSpot is the foundation; AI is built on top from day one.

**Path B — Already on HubSpot**
Flexible entry points depending on the customer's readiness:
- Audit only → clear assessment and roadmap, no further commitment required
- Audit + Pilot → prove value on 1–2 sales stages before full commitment
- Direct to full build → for customers who are ready and have a clean HubSpot setup

All paths converge on the same destination: full AI-powered sales across all five stages, maintained on a monthly retainer.

**Ongoing retainer**
The system is not a set-and-forget build. Buying signals need reviewing, scoring models need tuning, templates need updating as the offering evolves, and new HubSpot features need evaluating. The retainer is what keeps the system performing — typically 10–30 hours per month depending on scope.

## Tools & Technology

**Core platform**
- **HubSpot** — Sales Hub, Commerce Hub, and Smart CRM are the operational backbone. All AI features run within HubSpot.

**HubSpot AI (Breeze)**
- **Breeze Prospecting Agent** — automated prospecting, buying signal monitoring, personalised outreach
- **Company Research Agent** — deep account intelligence briefs, exportable as PDF
- **Breeze Data Agent** — custom research questions across the full CRM
- **Breeze Intelligence** — automatic contact and company enrichment
- **Breeze Assistant** — pre-call briefings, record summaries, call script and template generation
- **AI Lead Scoring** — predictive and rules-based qualification
- **CPQ with Breeze AI** — automated quote generation from deal context
- **AI Closing Agent** — 24/7 buyer support inside sent quotes
- **Smart Deal Progression** — post-call CRM updates, follow-up drafts, action items
- **Conversation Intelligence** — call transcription, analysis, and coaching insights

**Data providers (contact sourcing & enrichment)**
- Apollo, ZoomInfo, or Surfe — connected to HubSpot for contact sourcing and enrichment

**Person-level research**
- **Clay** — LinkedIn and multi-source contact enrichment, triggered on meeting bookings and synced back to HubSpot

**Automation & workflow**
- **make.com** — connecting tools and building automation flows that sit outside of HubSpot's native workflow builder where needed

**AI development**
- **Claude Code** — used internally for building custom automations, data processing, and any bespoke AI logic that goes beyond what HubSpot's native tools provide

## Team & Roles

A typical engagement involves three roles, sometimes carried by two people depending on scope:

**Sales strategist / consultant**
Owns the commercial system design. Works with the client to define ICP, selling profiles, qualification criteria, scoring model logic, and what "good" looks like at each stage of the sales process. This person understands the client's business and translates it into HubSpot and AI configuration decisions.

**HubSpot specialist**
Owns the technical implementation. Responsible for CRM architecture, pipeline setup, lifecycle stages, workflow builds, CPQ configuration, and ensuring the HubSpot foundation is solid enough for AI to run on top of.

**AI & automation specialist**
Configures the Breeze agents, connects data providers, builds Clay enrichment workflows, sets up make.com automation flows, and handles anything that requires custom AI logic via Claude Code. Responsible for the quality of the AI-powered outputs — outreach, briefings, quotes, closing responses.

**Account lead (retainer)**
Owns the ongoing relationship. Monitors system performance, identifies what needs tuning, manages the monthly retainer hours, and brings new HubSpot or AI capabilities to the client's attention as the platform evolves. May be the same person as the strategist.

## Process

### Path A — Full Implementation

**Week 1–2: Kickoff & discovery**
- Kickoff meeting with key stakeholders (sales lead, marketing owner, CRM admin if applicable)
- Deep-dive into the current sales process, team structure, and ICP
- Access provisioning: HubSpot account, data provider accounts, calendar integrations
- Data quality assessment: what's in the current CRM that can be migrated or used

**Week 2–4: HubSpot build**
- CRM architecture: contact and company properties, pipeline stages, lifecycle stages
- Product library: all offerings productised with correct pricing models
- Deal and contact data migrated or imported
- Core integrations connected (calendar, email, data provider)

**Week 4–7: AI layer build**
- Selling profile and buying profile created
- Prospecting Agent configured: signal monitoring, contact sourcing, outreach segmentation
- Breeze Intelligence activated and bulk enrichment run
- Lead scoring model designed and configured
- Company Research Agent deployed on company records
- Clay connected and meeting-triggered enrichment workflow built
- Call scripts and email templates built in Breeze Assistant
- CPQ configured: quote templates, approval rules, e-signature, payment setup
- Closing Agent knowledge base built
- Smart Deal Progression enabled and connected to pipeline definitions
- AI forecasting configured

**Week 7–8: Testing & training**
- Full system walkthrough with the sales team
- Test runs of each AI-powered stage with real accounts
- Rep training on the Sales Workspace, Prepare function, and AI-assisted workflows
- Final adjustments before go-live

**Week 8+: Go-live & retainer**
- System live across all five sales stages
- Monthly retainer begins: monitoring, tuning, and iteration

---

### Path B — Audit

**Week 1: Audit**
- Access to HubSpot account
- Assessment of: data quality, pipeline configuration, lifecycle stage logic, existing automations, and what's broken or missing
- Output: a clear written assessment and a prioritised roadmap for AI-powered sales

**Deliverable:** HubSpot audit report + AI-powered sales roadmap

---

### Path B — Pilot

**Week 1: Scoping**
- Based on audit findings (or discovery if no audit), agree on which 1–2 sales stages are in scope for the pilot
- Define success metrics for the pilot period

**Week 2–3: Build**
- Configure the AI layer for the agreed stages only
- Rep training on the specific tools in scope

**Weeks 3–12: Run & measure**
- System runs for 90 days
- Weekly or bi-weekly check-ins to review output quality and results
- Results documented against the agreed success metrics

**End of pilot: Review & proposal**
- Pilot debrief with stakeholders
- Results presented against success metrics
- Proposal for full build across remaining stages

---

### Path B — Full Build (post-audit or post-pilot)

Follows the same AI layer build and go-live process as Path A weeks 4–8, scoped to the stages not yet in place.

## Onboarding a New Client

The first two weeks set the tone for the whole engagement. The goal is to move fast enough to show momentum, and thorough enough to build something that actually works.

**Day 1: Access & alignment**
- HubSpot access granted to the delivery team
- Kickoff meeting booked with all key stakeholders
- Delivery timeline shared and agreed

**Week 1: Kickoff meeting**
The kickoff covers:
- The client's sales process end-to-end — how they currently prospect, qualify, prepare, quote, and close
- Their ICP in detail — who they sell to, what triggers a purchase, what objections come up
- CRM situation — what's in HubSpot, what's missing, what the team actually uses
- What success looks like in 90 days

**Week 1–2: Foundations**
- Data quality check run on existing HubSpot data
- Product library reviewed or built
- Selling profile and ICP/buying profile drafted and validated with the client
- Data provider connected and test enrichment run
- Calendar integrations connected for all reps

**First milestone: Prospecting Agent live**
The first visible output is the Prospecting Agent running on a real target account list with buying signal monitoring active. This is typically achievable within 2–3 weeks of kickoff and gives the client an early, tangible signal that the system is working.
