# What It Is — AI-Powered Sales

## Service Definition

AI-Powered Sales is a service that introduces AI into every key stage of the B2B sales process — from prospecting to closing — built on HubSpot as the commercial backbone. The service is designed for growth-oriented mid-market companies (€30–100M) in industrial, deep-tech, and B2B technology sectors with complex sales cycles and a team already doing structured selling.

We either build the full system from scratch alongside a HubSpot implementation, or layer AI-powered capabilities on top of an existing HubSpot setup. The result is a sales process where AI handles the admin, research, and follow-through — so reps spend their time on the conversations that actually close deals.

## What AI Does at Each Sales Stage

### Prospecting

#### What HubSpot's AI Does (Breeze Prospecting Agent)

HubSpot's **Breeze Prospecting Agent** automates three core functions:

**1. Buying signal monitoring** — Continuously watches enrolled accounts for signals that indicate purchase intent:
- Leadership changes
- Funding announcements
- Job postings (e.g. hiring a Head of Sales signals growth)
- Technology adoption changes
- Engagement spikes (form fills, page views, email opens)

**2. Contact sourcing & enrichment** — Integrates with third-party data providers (ZoomInfo, Apollo, Surfe) to find and enrich contacts with phone numbers, job titles, and company data. Contacts can be auto-enrolled via workflows or target account lists.

**3. Personalized outreach drafting** — Pulls from CRM data, web research, and detected signals to write personalized emails per contact. Two modes:
- **Review mode** — Rep approves each email before it sends
- **Autonomous mode** — Agent sends without human review once quality is trusted

Outreach can be segmented by product, market segment, or persona — so different messaging runs for different ICPs simultaneously.

**Key results (from HubSpot):**
- 28% increase in meetings booked (RevenueWell, replacing manual sequences)
- Up to 2x higher response rates vs. traditional sequences
- Processes 10 contacts/minute; up to 1,000 emails/day per account

#### What We Set Up for the Customer

This is not a plug-and-play tool — it requires significant configuration to perform well. Our service covers:

- **Selling profile creation** — Defining what the AI "knows" about the client's offering, ICP, tone of voice, and value propositions. This is the foundation that determines the quality of all outreach.
- **Buying profile creation** — Using HubSpot's Breeze "Infer company value proposition and ICP" workflow action to automatically generate structured buyer intelligence from a company's domain and website. Outputs include: problems the company solves, what they sell, target personas, and value propositions. These feed directly into the prospecting agent for personalised outreach. Capped at 3,000 executions/month.
- **Productising the client's offering in HubSpot** — Building out the client's product and service library in HubSpot (Commerce > Products) with correct naming, descriptions, pricing models (flat rate, tiered, recurring), and SKUs. This ensures the AI has structured, consistent product information to reference when drafting outreach — and also connects directly to quotes, deals, and invoices downstream.
- **Signal configuration** — Deciding which buying signals matter for this client's specific market and configuring monitoring accordingly.
- **Data provider integration** — Connecting and configuring ZoomInfo, Apollo, or Surfe within HubSpot.
- **Enrollment automation** — Building workflows and rulesets that automatically enroll the right contacts at the right time, with guardrails (exclude contacts already in active deals, sequences, etc.).
- **Outreach mode decision** — Advising on review vs. autonomous mode based on the client's risk tolerance and rep availability.
- **Segmentation** — Setting up separate profiles for different ICPs, products, or markets.
- **CRM data quality** — Ensuring the underlying contact and account data is clean and complete enough for AI to work from. Garbage in, garbage out.
- **Monitoring & iteration** — Reviewing signal triggers, outreach quality, and response rates over time and tuning accordingly.

#### What the Sales Rep Still Does

- Strategic relationship building and conversations
- Handling replies and booking meetings
- Deal closing
- Reviewing and approving AI-drafted emails (in review mode)


### Research & Qualification

#### What HubSpot's AI Does

HubSpot provides three interconnected tools for this stage: **Breeze Intelligence** (data enrichment), the **Company Research Agent** (deep account intelligence), and **AI Lead Scoring**.

**Company Research Agent (Breeze Studio) — Deep Account Research**

The Company Research Agent is a dedicated AI agent available in Breeze Studio and directly on company records in HubSpot. Given a company name or domain, it runs a full research sweep across public sources and internal CRM data and produces a structured intelligence brief.

Seven output sections per company:
- Executive Summary
- Company Overview & Background
- Recent News & Trigger Events
- Products & Services
- Industry & Competitive Landscape
- Customers & Market
- Company CRM Research (internal data)

Results can be exported as a **HubSpot Prospect Intelligence Brief PDF** — ready to share with a rep before a call.

The agent can be run manually from Breeze Studio (any company by name/domain) or embedded directly on company records (requires Sales Hub Professional or Enterprise). Super Admins can add custom instructions and automate the agent via Breeze Studio.

**Data Agent — Custom Research Questions at Scale**

The Breeze Data Agent goes beyond fixed fields — it answers custom business questions about contacts and companies across your entire CRM. It draws from CRM data, call recordings, email history, uploaded documents, and the web.

Example questions it can answer:
- "Which of our prospects are using a competitor's product?"
- "Which accounts had recent funding announcements?"
- "What pain points came up in calls with this account?"
- "Does this company have a dedicated sales team?"

Results flow back into CRM records, workflows, and Data Studio — so insights can trigger automations (e.g. enrol in a sequence when a competitor is mentioned).

Available from Starter tier upwards; runs on HubSpot Credits.

**Breeze Intelligence — Automatic Enrichment**

Once a contact or company is in HubSpot, Breeze Intelligence automatically enriches the record within seconds — no manual research needed. It draws from HubSpot's database of 200M+ company profiles plus third-party sources.

*Company-level data added:*
- Annual revenue range
- Employee count
- Industry classification
- Technology stack in use
- Funding levels
- Countries of operation

*Contact-level data added:*
- Job title and seniority level
- Department
- LinkedIn profile URL
- Location and time zone

This means reps enter every call with full company context already in the CRM — without spending time on manual pre-call research.

**AI Lead Scoring — Automatic Qualification**

HubSpot's AI analyzes closed-won, closed-lost, and open deals to identify which combinations of firmographic and behavioural signals are most predictive of conversion. It then automatically scores and ranks leads accordingly.

Signals analysed include:
- Profile fit (industry, company size, role, revenue)
- Behavioural signals (website visits, form submissions, email engagement, content consumption)
- Historical deal outcome patterns

Two models available:
- **Manual lead scoring** (Professional & Enterprise) — rules-based, defined by the team
- **Predictive lead scoring** (Enterprise only) — fully AI-driven, requires min. 100 customers and 1,000 non-customers of historical data

High-scoring leads are flagged for immediate follow-up; low-scoring leads can be routed to nurture tracks automatically.

#### What We Set Up for the Customer

- **Breeze Intelligence configuration** — Activating enrichment, setting up auto-enrichment on new contacts, and running bulk enrichment on existing CRM data. Deciding which fields to enrich and how to use them in workflows.
- **Lead scoring model design** — Defining the qualification criteria: which firmographic attributes signal a good fit (industry, company size, revenue), which behavioural signals indicate intent, and how to weight them.
- **AI lead scoring setup** — Configuring HubSpot's AI scoring model, selecting the evaluation timeframe, and monitoring the initial scoring run.
- **Qualification workflows** — Building automations that act on scores: routing high-fit leads to reps for immediate follow-up, enrolling low-fit leads in nurture sequences, and flagging leads that meet ICP criteria.
- **CRM lifecycle stage logic** — Defining what "qualified" means for this client and ensuring HubSpot's lifecycle stages (MQL, SQL) are tied to scoring thresholds, not just manual updates.
- **Enrichment-to-outreach connection** — Ensuring enriched data (tech stack, funding, headcount) flows into the prospecting agent's personalisation logic and into rep-facing views.
- **Call scripts & email templates** — Using Breeze Assistant to build AI-generated call scripts and email templates tailored to each ICP, lifecycle stage, and use case (e.g. initial outreach, reactivation, follow-up after no response). Templates live in HubSpot and are available to reps directly in their workflow — no switching tools. Scripts give reps the right talking points and objection handling based on the prospect's profile and research.

#### What the Sales Rep Still Does

- Final qualification judgment on edge cases
- Discovery conversations to validate AI-scored leads
- Updating deal stages based on qualification outcomes


### Meeting Preparation

#### What HubSpot's AI Does

HubSpot provides three tools that work together for meeting preparation: **Breeze Assistant pre-call briefing**, **Company Research Agent**, and **Conversation Intelligence**.

**Breeze Assistant — Pre-Call Briefing**

With a calendar connected, reps can hit "Prepare" on any upcoming meeting in HubSpot's Sales Workspace. Breeze Assistant generates a structured briefing that includes:
- Summaries of recent calls, emails, and website visits
- Company details and deal history
- Suggested pain points and opportunities to explore
- Key decision-makers across the account

Everything is pulled from live CRM data — no manual digging through notes or email threads before a call.

**Company Research Agent — Deep Account Brief**

Before a first meeting with a new account, the Company Research Agent can be run to produce a full intelligence brief (see Research & Qualification section). The output — covering recent news, funding signals, tech stack, competitive landscape, and products — can be exported as a PDF and shared with the rep before the call.

**Record Summarisation — Instant Context**

On any contact, company, or deal record, Breeze can generate a plain-language summary covering:
- Recent activities and notes
- Deal pipeline status and revenue potential
- Ownership and key properties

Available from any CRM subscription tier. Accessible directly from the record view or via Breeze Assistant chat.

**Conversation Intelligence — Learning from Past Calls**

Previous calls with the account are transcribed and analysed by AI (Sales Hub Professional/Enterprise). Reps can review past call summaries, key topics raised, objections, and competitor mentions before going back into the same account — so every meeting builds on the last.

**Contact-Level Research — Who You're Meeting**

Every meeting involves people, not just companies. HubSpot natively covers:
- Breeze Intelligence enrichment on the contact record (job title, seniority, department, LinkedIn URL)
- Contact record summary (past interactions, notes, open deals, last activity)
- Breeze Data Agent for custom questions (e.g. "what did this person say about budget on previous calls?")

However, HubSpot has a gap here — it doesn't offer deep person research: previous roles, career history, LinkedIn activity, or recent public content. This is where **Clay** fills the gap. Clay connects to 75+ data providers including LinkedIn, and syncs enriched person-level data back to HubSpot contact records automatically. A workflow can be built to trigger Clay enrichment whenever a meeting is booked, so the rep sees a fuller picture of each attendee before the call — role history, company background, LinkedIn profile — all inside HubSpot.

#### What We Set Up for the Customer

- **Calendar integration** — Connecting reps' calendars to HubSpot so the Prepare button and meeting assistant work automatically before every booked meeting.
- **Meeting preparation workflow** — Defining and standardising what a rep should review before each meeting type (discovery, demo, negotiation) and building Breeze Assistant prompts that surface the right context for each.
- **Company Research Agent deployment** — Installing the agent from Breeze Marketplace and embedding it on company records so reps can run a full account brief with one click, without leaving HubSpot.
- **Conversation Intelligence setup** — Enabling call recording and transcription, configuring AI analysis settings, and ensuring past call insights are visible and accessible before follow-up meetings.
- **Call scripts & talking point templates** — Building stage-specific call scripts in HubSpot (discovery, demo, follow-up) using Breeze Assistant, tailored to the client's ICP and common objections. Reps access these directly from the Sales Workspace.
- **Pre-meeting briefing templates** — Standardising what the AI briefing covers for each meeting type so reps always go in with consistent, relevant preparation — not just whatever happens to be on the record.
- **Clay integration for contact research** — Connecting Clay to HubSpot and building a workflow that triggers person-level enrichment when a meeting is booked. Clay pulls career history, LinkedIn profile, and background data from 75+ sources and writes it back to the HubSpot contact record — so reps know who they're meeting, not just what company they're from.

#### What the Sales Rep Still Does

- Reading and internalising the AI-generated briefing
- Adapting call scripts to the specific conversation
- Bringing relationship context that isn't in the CRM

### Offer Building

By this stage, the foundations are already in place: the product library is structured (from Prospecting), the contact and company data is enriched (from Research & Qualification), the deal record is populated from meetings and Smart Deal Progression, and call transcripts capture what the prospect actually cares about. Offer building pulls all of this together into a professional, personalised quote — automatically.

#### What HubSpot's AI Does

**HubSpot CPQ with Breeze AI — Automated Quote Generation**

HubSpot's CPQ (Configure, Price, Quote) tool in Commerce Hub uses Breeze AI to auto-draft personalised quotes directly from deal context. A rep can prompt it conversationally: *"Generate a quote for [contact] at [company] using our standard service template"* — and the AI retrieves the contact, company details, and relevant products from the library and composes the quote within seconds.

What the AI-generated quote includes:
- Line items pulled from the product library (set up in the Prospecting phase)
- Pricing models (flat rate, tiered, recurring) — pre-configured per product
- Discounts applied based on deal stage or lifecycle rules
- Tax rates at line-item level
- Branded templates (logo, colours, fonts)
- Custom modules e.g. "Why Work With Us", case study links, terms & conditions
- Personalisation tokens drawing from CRM contact and company data

**Smart Approval Workflows**

Approval rules trigger automatically based on quote criteria — e.g. manager sign-off required if discount exceeds 20% or deal value is above a threshold. Reps get instant feedback and can adjust on the spot without back-and-forth email chains.

**Buyer Engagement Tracking**

Once sent, HubSpot tracks in real time when the prospect opens the quote and which sections they spend time on. Reps are notified at peak engagement moments — the optimal time to follow up.

**E-Signature & Payment Collection**

Quotes include embedded e-signature. Once signed, the deal stage updates to Closed Won automatically. Integrated with HubSpot Payments and Stripe for payment collection directly from the quote.

#### What We Set Up for the Customer

- **CPQ configuration** — Setting up Commerce Hub CPQ, connecting the product library built in the Prospecting phase, and configuring pricing rules, discount logic, and tax rates.
- **Quote templates** — Building branded quote templates in HubSpot that reflect the client's visual identity and include the right custom modules for their offering (service descriptions, case studies, terms).
- **Approval workflow rules** — Defining the logic for when approvals are required and who approves, and building the workflow so it triggers automatically.
- **Smart Deal Progression connection** — Ensuring call transcript insights and deal field updates (from the Meeting Preparation phase) feed into the quote so the AI has accurate, up-to-date deal context to work from.
- **Buyer engagement notifications** — Setting up alerts and follow-up workflows triggered when a prospect opens or engages with a quote — so reps act at the right moment, not days later.
- **E-signature and payment setup** — Configuring e-signature, connecting HubSpot Payments or Stripe, and automating the deal stage update when a quote is signed.

#### What the Sales Rep Still Does

- Reviewing and adjusting the AI-drafted quote before sending
- Deciding on pricing and discount strategy for each deal
- Following up when buyer engagement is detected
- Negotiating terms


### Closing

At this stage the quote is sent and the deal is in the prospect's hands. The risk here is momentum loss — slow replies to buyer questions, missed follow-up moments, deals stalling without anyone noticing. HubSpot's AI addresses exactly this: keeping deals moving, surfacing risk early, and ensuring the rep acts at the right moment.

#### What HubSpot's AI Does

**AI Closing Agent — 24/7 Buyer Support Inside the Quote**

The Closing Agent lives directly inside the sent quote. When a buyer has a question — about pricing, product details, contract terms, or legal clauses — they get an instant answer without having to wait for the rep to respond. The agent draws from knowledge the client provides: product catalogue, pricing rules, sales collateral, and pre-approved legal documents.

This handles micro-negotiations before they become blockers. A buyer reviewing a quote at 9pm on a Friday no longer has to wait until Monday for a basic question to be answered. The path from proposal to signature stays clear.

**Smart Deal Progression — AI After Every Call**

After every call between a rep and a prospect, Smart Deal Progression analyses the meeting transcript against the full deal history — past emails, notes, deal activity, prior interactions — and produces three outputs:
- **Suggested CRM updates** — deal stage, amount, close date, next steps, conversation outcome
- **Drafted follow-up email** — personalised, ready to review and send
- **Action item list** — what needs to happen next and by whom

It applies business context from the client's pipeline definitions and forecasting logic, and aligns suggestions with how their team actually executes. This keeps deal records accurate and ensures nothing falls through the cracks between meetings. (Public beta from April 2026.)

**Buyer Engagement Tracking — Act at the Right Moment**

When a prospect opens the quote and engages with specific sections, the rep is notified in real time. This turns follow-up from guesswork into a signal-driven action — reaching out when interest is highest, not a day after it peaked.

**AI Deal Forecasting & Pipeline Risk Detection**

HubSpot's AI analyses historical closed-won and closed-lost deal data to assign a **Likelihood to Close** score to every open deal. Forecasting provides three monthly projections (most likely, upper, lower bounds), updated on days 1, 7, 14, 21, and 28 of each month. Workflows can monitor for score drops and automatically trigger rep tasks — so deals at risk are flagged before the forecast meeting, not during it.

**E-Signature & Automated Deal Closure**

When the buyer signs the quote, the deal stage updates to Closed Won automatically. Payment collection via HubSpot Payments or Stripe is embedded in the same flow.

#### What We Set Up for the Customer

- **Closing Agent configuration** — Building the knowledge base the agent draws from: product catalogue, pricing rules, FAQ responses, and pre-approved legal/contract language. This determines the quality of answers the buyer receives.
- **Smart Deal Progression enablement** — Activating the feature, connecting it to the client's pipeline definitions and deal stages, and training the team on reviewing and acting on AI-suggested updates and follow-ups.
- **Buyer engagement notification workflows** — Building automations that alert the rep and trigger a follow-up task the moment a prospect engages with a sent quote.
- **AI forecasting setup** — Configuring HubSpot's AI forecasting, ensuring deal data quality (the accuracy of forecasts depends entirely on clean deal records), and building pipeline risk workflows that flag deals with falling likelihood scores.
- **Deal stage automation** — Connecting e-signature to automatic deal stage updates and closed-won triggers so pipeline data stays accurate without manual updates.
- **Closing playbooks** — Using Breeze Assistant to build rep-facing closing playbooks in HubSpot: objection handling guides, negotiation talking points, and final-stage email templates — all accessible from the deal record.

#### What the Sales Rep Still Does

- Strategic negotiation and relationship management
- Reviewing and sending AI-drafted follow-up emails
- Acting on buyer engagement signals
- Final decision-making on deal terms and discounts

## What This Is Not

- **Not a HubSpot implementation project** — HubSpot setup may be part of the engagement, but the service is a commercial system built on top of it, not a CRM deployment.
- **Not a lead generation campaign** — AI-Powered Sales is a system-level engagement that changes how the entire sales process operates. It is not a tactic for getting more leads.
- **Not sales automation that replaces reps** — AI handles admin, research, and follow-through. Reps still own relationships, conversations, negotiation, and closing. The goal is to give them more time and better information, not to remove them.
- **Not a one-off project** — the system requires ongoing monitoring, tuning, and iteration. A single implementation without a retainer will degrade over time.
